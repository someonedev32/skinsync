Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 19, 2026

1 – Introduction

AI SkinSync (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application AI SkinSync. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2 – Information We Collect

2.1 – Device & Usage Data

We automatically collect technical and usage information about your device and how you use the App, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address and network data
  • App usage and interaction logs

2.2 – Face Photos and Analysis Data

With your voluntary permission, the App collects and uploads facial photos for skin analysis. These photos are processed securely over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and analyzed to detect non-biometric skin attributes. We do NOT collect or store facial geometry, faceprints, embeddings, or any identifiers capable of recognizing a person. The photo is used solely for analysis purposes and results are generated without retaining the actual biometric data.

3 – How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App’s features
  • To analyze photos and generate personalized skin results
  • To improve our services and app performance
  • To communicate with users who contact support

4 – Photo Uploads and Analysis

When you submit a selfie for analysis, the image is transmitted over secure encrypted connections to our backend for processing. We analyze the image only to derive non-biometric skin attributes (e.g., pores, wrinkles, acne). We do NOT store any facial identifiers or use them for any purpose other than generating your analysis.

5 – Data Storage and Retention

Photos and analysis results may be stored in our encrypted servers to support app features such as history. Each stored photo can be deleted by the user at any time through the App’s history feature. If the user deletes their account or uninstalls the App, all associated photos and analysis data will be deleted within 90 days.

6 – Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share the information collected with trusted third-party service providers who assist in delivering app features (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics) under confidentiality obligations. Your face photos are not shared with third parties for commercial purposes.

7 – Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

8 – Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy within the App and on our website. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

9 – Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can contact us by email at support@inovation.app.
