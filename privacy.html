Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 19, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how AI SkinSync ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the AI SkinSync mobile application ("the App"). By accessing or using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Device & Usage Data

We automatically collect certain technical information about your device and how you use the App. This may include:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Unique identifiers (e.g., device ID)
  • Usage patterns and analytics
  • IP address and network data

2.2 Facial Photos for Analysis

With your explicit permission, the App collects facial photos for skin analysis. These photos are processed securely over encrypted connections and used solely for generating results. The App does not collect or store biometric identifiers such as faceprints, facial geometry, or other uniquely identifying facial data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the features of the App
  • To analyze skin attributes and generate personalized results
  • For analytics and app performance monitoring
  • To communicate with you if you contact support

4. Face Data & Photo Processing

Facial photos you upload are used to derive analysis results (e.g., skin traits like pores, wrinkles, acne) and are transmitted over secure TLS/HTTPS connections to our servers. We do not retain any facial geometry or identifiers capable of recognizing an individual.

Photos processed by the service may be stored temporarily for basic analysis and display within the App history feature, and can be deleted by the user at any time. If an account is deleted or the App is uninstalled, all associated analysis data and photos will be removed from our servers within a reasonable time.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information and usage data only as long as necessary to provide the App’s services and to fulfill operational or legal requirements. Data can be removed upon user request where applicable.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share information with third-party service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics partners) solely to support App functionality and in compliance with this Privacy Policy.

7. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect the data we collect. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal data. You may contact us at any time to exercise these rights.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. We will notify users of material changes by posting the updated policy within the App or on the App Store/Play Store listing.

10. Contact Us
