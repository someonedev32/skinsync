Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how AI SkinSync (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) handles information when you use our mobile application.

Short summary: AI SkinSync is designed to minimize data collection. Photos used for cosmetic skin analysis are processed in real time and are not stored by default. Optional before/after photos and habit/progress data can be stored locally on your device to support app features.

1) What AI SkinSync Does

AI SkinSync provides cosmetic and informational tools such as: cosmetic skin analysis scores, progress tracking, daily tips, routines, habit tracking (e.g., water), goals, reminders, and product recommendations. The app does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

2) Camera & Photos

2.1 Cosmetic Skin Analysis

  • The app may use your device camera to capture a photo for cosmetic skin analysis (e.g., dryness, redness, oiliness, texture, dark spots).
  • Photos used for analysis are processed in real time and are not stored by the app.
  • Analysis photos are not shared with third parties for advertising or tracking.

2.2 Before / After Photos (Optional)

  • If you choose to use the Before/After feature, you may save photos for personal visual comparison.
  • These photos are stored only on your device (local storage).
  • We do not upload Before/After photos to our servers.
  • You can remove them by deleting them in the app (if available) or by uninstalling the app.

3) Local Data Stored on Your Device

To provide core functionality, the app may store certain information locally on your device, such as:

  • Scan results and progress history (numeric scores and timestamps)
  • Daily tips history (if implemented)
  • Water tracker entries and targets
  • Goals, streaks, and routine completion data
  • Reminder preferences
  • Entitlement state for scan access (e.g., whether the free scan was used, last scan date, scan credits)

This data is used to show trends and improve your in-app experience and is stored locally unless explicitly stated otherwise.

4) Payments (In-App Purchases)

AI SkinSync may offer optional in-app purchases (for example, additional weekly scans). Payments are processed by Apple via In-App Purchase. We do not collect your payment card details.

5) Product Recommendations & Affiliate Disclosure

The app may show recommended skincare products and provide links to external product pages. Some links may be affiliate links, meaning we may earn a commission if you purchase through those links, at no extra cost to you. When you open an external link, that website’s privacy practices apply.

6) Analytics (Optional)

We may collect anonymous usage data to understand app performance and improve user experience. This data is not intended to identify you and is not used for targeted advertising.

7) Information We Do Not Collect

AI SkinSync does not collect or store:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses (unless you contact us directly)
  • Health or medical records (e.g., HealthKit/clinical records)
  • Precise or coarse location
  • Payment card numbers (payments are handled by Apple)
  • Contacts/address book data

8) Permissions & Your Choices

  • Camera: You can deny or revoke camera access in your device settings. The scan feature requires camera permission.
  • Notifications: You can enable or disable reminders in device settings.
  • Deleting local data: Uninstalling the app removes locally stored app data from your device.

9) Security

We aim to minimize data handling. Optional saved content (such as Before/After photos or habit tracking) is stored locally on your device.

10) Children’s Privacy

AI SkinSync is not intended for children. If you believe a child has provided information to us, contact us and we will address it.

11) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the app’s features or data practices. The updated version will be posted on this page.
