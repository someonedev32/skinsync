Privacy Policy

SkinSync values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the SkinSync mobile application.

Information We Use

SkinSync uses access to your device camera to allow you to take a photo for cosmetic skin analysis.

  • Photos are used only for real-time skin analysis.
  • Photos are not stored, not saved, and not shared.
  • Photos are not linked to your identity.

Information We Do Not Collect

SkinSync does not collect, store, or process:

  • Personal contact information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Health or medical data
  • Biometric identifiers
  • Location data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Contacts or address book data

Analytics

SkinSync may collect anonymous usage data such as interactions with features in the app for the purpose of improving performance and user experience. This data is not linked to any individual user and is not used for tracking.

Purpose of the App

SkinSync provides cosmetic and informational skin analysis and skincare product recommendations only. The app does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Data Security

Because SkinSync does not store user photos or personal data, there is no personal data retained on our servers.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
